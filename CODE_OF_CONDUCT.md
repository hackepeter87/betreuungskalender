# Code of Conduct

## Our standards

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, discuss
technical decisions in good faith, accept constructive review, and protect the
privacy of all people represented by application data.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, threats,
publishing another person's private information, or using project discussions
to assess or accuse people involved in a family matter.

## Scope and enforcement

These rules apply in repository discussions, issues, reviews, and other project
spaces. Maintainers may edit or remove content and restrict participation when
necessary to protect contributors or affected individuals.

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository's maintainer contact
or GitHub reporting features. Do not include unrelated personal family data.
